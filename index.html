The Problem

AI is making regulated decisions. Nobody can prove how.

FCA-regulated firms deploying AI in investment advice, portfolio management, and client communications face an uncomfortable truth: when the regulator asks why the AI made that recommendation, there is no answer. The model is a black box. The decision is gone. The liability is real.
