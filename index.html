The Problem

AI is making regulated decisions. Nobody can prove how.

When the FCA requests evidence of how an AI-assisted recommendation was generated, most firms cannot produce it. The decision payload is gone, the reasoning is unrecorded, and the liability is real.

01 No audit trail
AI decisions are made and lost with no cryptographic record of what input produced what output.
02 Black-box liability
Without decision records, firms cannot demonstrate compliance under FCA PS22/3 or Consumer Duty.
03 Logging gaps
Application logs capture operational telemetry. They do not produce the structured records compliance requires.
04 August 2026 deadline
EU AI Act Article 12 enforcement begins. Firms without certified audit trails are not ready.

How It Works

From AI decision to signed certificate in under 2 seconds.

01
Submit decision payload
Your AI system sends a decision payload to a single REST endpoint. No changes to your model or infrastructure required.
02
PII redaction
Personal data is automatically stripped before storage. GDPR compliance is built in, not bolted on.
03
Cryptographic signing
A certificate is generated using HMAC-SHA256 and sealed with Cloud KMS. Tamper-proof and independently verifiable.
04
Immutable storage
Written to an append-only, WORM-locked ledger with 7-year retention. Immutable. Court-ready. Auditable.
