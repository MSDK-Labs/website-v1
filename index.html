The Problem

AI is making regulated decisions. Nobody can prove how.

Financial institutions are deploying AI across portfolio management, risk assessment, and client advisory — yet most lack any mechanism to record, sign, or reproduce the reasoning behind each decision. When the FCA or EU regulators come knocking, firms face an impossible task: proving compliance for systems that were never built to explain themselves.
