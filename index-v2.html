How it works

From AI decision to signed certificate.

01
Capture
Your system sends a structured decision payload to a single REST endpoint.
02
Process
Personal data fields are tokenised based on configurable schema rules.
03
Sign
Payload is hashed and signed using HMAC-SHA256 with managed key infrastructure.
04
Store
Certificate written to append-only, WORM-locked store with defined retention.
